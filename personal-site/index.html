A little about Sherelton

Sherelton was born in Ganta City, Nimba County, Republic of Liberia on the 24th of March, 1996 to the Union of Mr. & Mrs. Garty Bartuah Dennis. He has eight sisters, seven older ones and one younger one. He is the second to the last sibling. He begins his primary education in Zayglay Town, Zayglay Catholic School System. He attended the Centre Virginia Public School beginning in 2009 where he took the National Exam [WASE] for 9th Grade in the year 2010 at Bomblim Girls Mission Campus in Clay-Island, Monrovia. He graduated in 2018 August from The Soul-Clinic Public High School [Uncle Sam] and is now a student at Brigham Young University in Idaho, United States of America, studying Web and Computer Programming. Sherelton is single and has a boy child aged 3-4 years
