Bio

Sherelton is a member of the LDS Church. He joined The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints on May 13, 2013, in Browersville City, Monrovia. He served as a full-time Missionary in Lagos Nigeria, and Abuja for the Church in the year May 18, 2018, to 2021. After being a member for 6 years, he has served many positions in the church, including; Ward Missionary Leader, Ward Executive Secretory, Young Men President, Quorum First Counselor, Young Single Adult Representative, Institute Instructor, and Seminaries Instructor. He is currently serving as Elder Quorum First Counselor and Supervisor for the Institute and Seminaries of Religions in Morris-Farm Ward. He is a student at Brigham Young University in Idaho, USA
