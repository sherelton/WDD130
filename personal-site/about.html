Note

Sherelton was born in Ganta City, Nimba County, Republic of Liberia on the 24th of March, 1996 to the Union of Mr. & Mrs. Garty Bartuah DennisSherelton is a member of the LDS Church. In his early life, Sherelton grows up in two churches due to his both parents having a different religion. He attended The Liberia Inland Church of God from his Moher side while he also goes to the Romans Catholic Church many times doing his primary year in school. His father was one of the leaders. Sherelton later joined The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints on May 13, 2013, in Browerville City, Monrovia. He served as a full-time Missionary in Lagos Nigeria, and Abuja for the Church in the year May 18, 2018, to 2021.He begins his primary education in Zayglay Town, Zayglay Catholic School System. He attended the Centre Virginia Public School beginning in 2009 where he took the National Exam [WASE] for 9th Grade in the year 2010 at Bomblim Girls Mission Campus in Clay-Island, Monrovia. He graduated in 2018 August from The Soul-Clinic Public High School [Uncle Sam] and is now a student at Brigham Young University in Idaho, United States of America, studying Web and Computer Programming.

Note

After being a member for 6 years, he has served many positions in the church, including; Ward Missionary Leader, Ward Executive Secretory, Young Men President, Quorum First Counselor, Young Single Adult Representative, Institute Instructor, and Seminaries Instructor.
